About Us

Shree Sanwariya Palace and Restaurant, nestled in the heart of Madhya Pradesh, combines luxury, comfort, and elegance to offer an unforgettable experience. With premium accommodations, a celebrated vegetarian menu, and a welcoming ambiance, we aim to provide unparalleled hospitality and memorable moments for every guest.

Shree Sanwariya Palace is easily accessible and serves as a gateway to explore the region's beauty and culture. Surrounded by serene landscapes and vibrant local attractions, our guests enjoy the perfect balance of tranquility and excitement. The strategic location also makes our palace an ideal base for business travelers, offering convenience and connectivity alongside luxury. Founded and led by Arjun Singh Mewada, Shree Sanwariya Palace reflects his vision of creating a sanctuary that harmonizes comfort, elegance, and heartfelt service. His passion for hospitality and dedication to excellence is evident in every aspect of the palace, from its thoughtfully designed spaces to its well-curated services. Shree Sanwariya Palace and Restaurant invites you to experience the finest hospitality in Madhya Pradesh. Whether you're seeking a luxurious stay, a gastronomic journey, or a venue for your cherished celebrations, we look forward to welcoming you and crafting memories that last a lifetime. Come and discover the art of refined living with us.
